What the product is today, what it becomes in twelve months, and what is on the table for year two and beyond.

What stays out of scope — year 1

Things we are NOT building in the next 12 months, even if they sound like good ideas:
  • Practice-management integrations (ezyVet, IDEXX, etc.)
  • Lab / imaging import
  • Treatment / prescribing layer
  • Multi-vet collaboration on a case
  • Research / academic dataset offerings
  • SOC 2 / ISO certifications
  • Voice-driven exam / image-based parameter extraction
  • Languages beyond PT and EN
  • Apple Watch / wearables
  • Native desktop apps (the PWA covers desktop browsers)

Each of those is a year-two or year-three conversation. They are listed here so we collectively resist the temptation to build them when a single paying user asks. Year-one discipline is the price of getting to 200 accounts.
