Plan — year 1

12-month plan · target: 200 paying accounts · status: draft

One single objective for year one: 200 paying accounts at ~$10/month, on a launched and self-running product. Below is the path — divided into three phases, each with its own milestone.

Phases

PhaseMonthsOutcome
P1 — Build M1 – M5 Brand, mobile app (PWA + native wrappers), backend, payments, legal docs, app store accounts, content tooling. End of P1: a real payment completes end-to-end on a real device, store submissions are in flight, the AI-clone content pipeline can produce a video.
P2 — Soft launch M6 Early-bird offer goes out to the Instagram audience. End of P2: first paying-account cohort, real feedback collected, top issues fixed.
P3 — Grow, iterate, record numbers M7 – M12 Six months of content scaling via marketing tools and services, retention work, native app stores live, regional pricing tuned. Every metric tracked weekly. End of P3: 200 paying accounts, churn understood, ready for either year-two structure or VC pitch.

Budget — year 1

LineCostNotes
Bemjax all-in fee€2,000 / month × 12 = €24,000 (+ tax)Covers everything Bemjax delivers: development, infrastructure (hosting, database, monitoring, email, CDN, certificates), funnel tooling (landing page, email platform, analytics), app store accounts (Apple Developer, Google Play), domains, legal templates, baseline brand assets, marketing tools and services, strategy and advisory.
AI API (LLM)variable, pass-throughThe only cost outside the €2k. Scales with case volume and prompt size. Tracked monthly; passed through at cost, no markup.
Year-one total (cash)€24,000 + AI API + taxThe Bemjax fee is fixed and predictable. AI API is the one variable line. Tax is added on top of the fee per local rules.
What the €2k covers. All Bemjax delivery — dev, servers, infrastructure, monthly tooling, app store accounts, domains, legal templates, brand baseline. We do not invoice these separately. The only cost on top is the variable AI API bill, because it scales with usage.
Break-even. 200 paying accounts × $10/month = $2,000/month gross. That matches the Bemjax fee at parity. AI API runs on top of that as a variable line. True sustainability — fee + AI API + healthy margin — sits closer to 300–500 accounts. 200 is the year-one floor: if we hit it, the funnel works and scale-up is year-two work.

Risk register

RiskLikelihoodMitigation
Instagram audience converts below 1% Medium Tight feedback loop on content cadence and the early-bird offer. If 100 accounts is not in sight by month 9 (3 months into the grow phase), the funnel is wrong — we re-design before pouring more content into it.
Apple App Store rejection cycle drags Medium Launch as PWA first. App store native is a second push, not a launch dependency.
Ronaldo's time becomes the bottleneck High at start Marketing tools and services for content production, Ivana for day-to-day product / support. Ronaldo's involvement is bounded after the initial recording session.
LGPD / regulatory question on "diagnostic decision support" Low Disclaimer + classification confirmation from CRMV before launch. Decision support tools are typically not classified as medical devices, but we confirm in writing.
Hidden costs on mobile (Apple 30% cut, Stripe fees) High Web-first pricing (Stripe) reduces cut to ~3%. Native app price set higher to absorb the 30%, with messaging that web is cheaper.
Year-one revenue does not cover year-two cash flow Medium Year-two structure renegotiated at month 12 from real data. Options: revised cash split, switch to revenue share, or VC raise. We do not commit to year two now.

Year-end decision tree

Year 2 default structure is already agreed: 15% Bemjax equity + €2k/month (continued). The tree below decides whether we activate it, modify it, or stop.

Where we land at month 12What we do
≥ 200 paying accounts, healthy retentionYear 2 activates as planned (15% + €2k). Optional VC pitch using the year-one numbers as proof.
100–199 accountsYear 2 extends but we discuss whether the equity escalation timing should slide and / or scope adjusts. Focus shifts to retention + referral.
< 100 accountsYear-end review. Pivot the offer (price, target audience, product), wind down with what we have learnt, or part ways cleanly with the IP staying with Ivana + Ronaldo.
