Content strategy — Instagram funnel + content scaling

How 30k+ followers become 200 paying accounts in 12 months

The asset

Two Instagram accounts: ~27k Brazilian followers, ~7k international. Predominantly practicing veterinarians. This is the entire growth channel for year one — we are not running ads, we are not doing SEO at scale.

Conversion target: roughly 0.6–1% of the audience over twelve months. That is a realistic figure for an authority-led niche product with a free trial — not a commodity launch.

The constraint

Ronaldo's time is the bottleneck. Clinical work, teaching, conferences. We cannot build a content cadence that depends on him being on camera every week. The whole funnel breaks if we try.

Solution: marketing tools and services that produce video content in Ronaldo's voice and likeness from a short source recording. The bulk of the cadence runs through them. Ivana writes the scripts. Ronaldo reviews for clinical accuracy and records the source material once.

How content scaling works

The chosen marketing stack reads scripts in Ronaldo's voice and likeness from a short source recording. The output is convincing PT-BR video at a fraction of the time cost of doing each video live. This is not a deepfake gimmick — it is content scaling for a domain expert with limited hours.

Specific tools and pricing are decided as part of the stack-selection work — Ivana evaluates the available options (candidates today include HeyGen, Synthesia, and D-ID on the video side, ElevenLabs on the voice side) against PT-BR quality, lip-sync naturalness, and medical-term pronunciation, and picks the combination that fits.

Source recording session

One-time session with Ronaldo. ~1 hour total.

  • Video — 2–5 min direct to camera, neutral background, even lighting. Multiple expressions if the tool supports it.
  • Voice — 10–30 min clean audio. Reading scripts is fine. Cover the medical terminology that will appear repeatedly.
  • Approval — Ronaldo reviews the generated avatar before any public use. If it does not look or sound like him, we adjust. He has veto.

Disclosure

Be transparent. "Content created with AI, clinical knowledge by Dr. Ronaldo" or equivalent label on AI-generated posts. The vet community values authenticity. Hidden AI use, if discovered, would damage Ronaldo's reputation badly — not worth it. Instagram is also tightening rules on undisclosed AI faces.

Each stage has its own break-fix. If awareness is fine but activation is poor — the product is hard to start with. If activation is fine but conversion is poor — the free-trial value is not clear or pricing is wrong. We watch all six stages weekly.

Early-bird launch play

The single most important content moment in year one is the launch + early-bird offer. Plan:

  1. Pre-launch teasers — Ronaldo posts "something I have been working on" with a short screen recording. Ivana cross-posts. Goal: build a DM list of warm leads we can message directly on launch day.
  2. Launch post — full case walkthrough (Reel + carousel). "I built this tool I use in my practice. Sharing it with you."
  3. Early-bird offer — discount on the launch price for the first cohort (see pricing.html). Discount level and cohort size decided closer to launch.
  4. DM the warm list — Ronaldo personally replies / DMs the people who showed early interest, with the early-bird link.
  5. Reply to every comment — for the first days after launch, every comment gets a reply. Highest-converting activity that exists at this stage.

What does not work — known traps

  • Posting and hoping. Without a clear DM-and-respond loop, content generates likes but not conversion. Reply work is non-negotiable.
  • AI-content fatigue. If every post is produced via the marketing stack, the audience notices. Mix in occasional real-Ronaldo content.
  • Over-explaining the product. Vets do not want feature lists; they want to see a real case solved faster than they could solve it themselves.
  • Pivoting content style every week. Pick a format, run it for at least 4 weeks before judging it.
  • Letting Ronaldo do the support work. Clinical questions: yes. App-bug-report DMs: route to Ivana / Dario. Protect his hours.
