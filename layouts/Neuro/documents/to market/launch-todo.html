Launch TODO — what a launch needs

Operational checklist · grouped by area · gates marked where one item must finish before the next can start

Roles: Dario Bemjax / dev Ivana product / content Ronaldo domain / personal brand All joint decision

1. Brand & foundation

  • All Brainstorm 5–10 name candidates
    • Two words max, easy to spell in PT and EN
    • Avoid generic "VetNeuro"-style names that cannot be trademarked
  • Dario Check availability for each candidate (.com, .com.br, Instagram handle, App Store, Google Play)
  • All Pick one available everywhere
  • Dario Register .com + .com.br domains
  • Dario Reserve Instagram brand handle
  • Dario Logo — square icon, horizontal, favicon
  • Dario Landing page (1-pager, mobile-first, PT primary / EN secondary)
    • Hero + tagline, three key features, "who built this", email signup
    • Email capture into Buttondown or Mailchimp free tier
  • Ronaldo "About" copy — why the tool exists, personal credibility
Gate A — landing page live, brand decided. P1 starts.

2. Mobile app + backend

  • Dario Repackage Neuro layout as PWA — installable on iOS + Android home screen, offline-capable for the parameters/localisation flow
  • Dario Mobile-first UX pass on Parameters and Localisation apps (one-handed, large tap targets, scroll behaviour)
  • Dario User accounts + auth (email + password, magic link as alternative)
  • Dario Subscription state model — trial / active / past-due / cancelled, persisted to backend
  • Dario Case storage — vet enters parameters, can save cases, retrieve later
  • Dario AI agent layer — wraps the deterministic engine, answers in natural language but cannot output anything outside the engine's localisations / diagnoses
  • Dario Server + database — domain, certificate, managed Postgres, automated backups
  • Dario Monitoring — uptime + error alerting (Sentry or similar)

3. Payments

  • Dario Stripe account (business info, bank account)
  • Dario Subscription billing via Stripe Checkout or Payment Links
  • Dario Customer portal — change card, cancel, view invoices
  • Dario End-to-end test: signup → trial → charge → cancel → reactivate
  • Dario Google Play Billing for Android native — subscription product configured. (Apple in-app purchase / StoreKit is year-2 work, when the iOS native ships.)
  • All Decide regional pricing (BRL for Brazilian market vs USD/EUR for international)

4. App store accounts & submission — year 1 (Google Play only)

  • Dario Google Play Developer account ($25 one-time) — identity verification (1–7 days)
  • Dario App store assets — 1024² + 512² icons, screenshots, short + full description, ASO keywords, Medical / Education category
  • Ronaldo App descriptions in PT and EN — emphasise "built by a veterinary neurologist", include searchable phrases (veterinary neurology, neuro exam, differential diagnosis)
  • Dario Privacy Policy URL live before submission
  • Dario Demo account credentials for the Play reviewer
  • Dario Submit to Google Play
PWA + Google Play are the year-1 distribution vehicles. Apple App Store / iOS native is year-2 work — Apple Developer enrolment, DUNS, App Store Connect, StoreKit subscriptions and the Apple submission flow are all deferred. Detail in publish-ios.html.

5. Legal & compliance

  • Dario In-app disclaimer — "diagnostic aid, does not replace clinical judgment / physical exam / complementary exams". On first launch (must accept), in app footer, on landing page.
  • Dario Terms of Service (Termly / TermsFeed template, ~€10–50). PT + EN. Limitation of liability, no diagnostic guarantee, subscription / cancellation terms.
  • Dario Privacy Policy — LGPD-compliant. Lists data collected, storage, third-party sharing (analytics, payments), user rights (access, deletion, portability).
  • Ronaldo Confirm with CRMV that "diagnostic decision support tool" does not require medical-device registration — get the response in writing
  • Ronaldo Review all user-facing copy for clinical accuracy — never "diagnose", always "assist / suggest / support"
  • All Founder agreement signed — equity split, cash terms, IP ownership, exit clauses, what happens at month 12
  • All Decide business entity — who legally owns the app (LLC, partnership, individual), where it is registered
Gate B — payments work end-to-end, app passes Apple review (or PWA is acceptable), legal docs live. Launch becomes possible.

6. Pre-launch content & teasers

  • Ronaldo Source recording session for content scaling — direct-to-camera + clean voice, neutral background
  • Ivana Evaluate marketing tools and services for content production; configure the chosen stack with Ronaldo's source material
  • Ivana Tune the chosen stack for PT-BR output, including medical-term pronunciation
  • Ronaldo Approve produced content — accuracy of representation, voice match, comfort with public use
  • Ivana Content production workflow — Ivana writes scripts, Ronaldo reviews, Ivana generates & posts
  • All AI disclosure policy — transparent label on AI-produced content (e.g. "content created with AI, clinical knowledge by Dr. Ronaldo")
  • Ronaldo Pre-launch teaser — 15–30 sec screen recording, "Something I have been working on", BR account first then global
  • Ivana Early-interest collection — Google Form or DM list, count and tag warm leads

7. Launch day

  • Ronaldo Launch post — full case walkthrough Reel (60–90s) + carousel screenshots. "I built this tool I use in my practice. Sharing it with you."
  • Ronaldo Stories with early-bird offer — discount on launch price for the first cohort, link to signup
  • Ivana Supporting post from her own account — "as a vet who has been testing this..."
  • Dario Real-time monitoring — signups, server load, payment flow, error tracking
  • Dario On standby for hot-fixes — first 24h are critical
  • Ronaldo Reply personally to every DM and comment on launch day — highest-converting activity that exists

8. First two weeks after launch

  • Ronaldo 2–3 more case walkthroughs across the week, varied conditions
  • Ivana Feedback form to every new user at day 3 — "how is it going? what is missing?"
  • Dario Track signups, trial → paid conversion, daily active users
  • Dario Ship top 3 reported issues within the first week
  • All Daily 15-min standup (WhatsApp or quick call) — numbers, issues, feedback
  • Ivana Tag the most active early users — they become testimonials and referral sources

9. What we will not do (year 1)

  • Hire a marketing agency.
  • Spend on paid ads.
  • Build a feature factory before launch — we ship a focused product and iterate on real cases.
  • Over-invest in branding / logo design beyond a clean baseline.
  • Take on enterprise / clinic-license deals before the consumer product is stable.
  • Promise compliance certifications we do not have (HIPAA, ISO 13485, FDA / Anvisa medical-device).
